Pick (temporary name) will help you to pick the right tool for you.
Pick is using the crowd, developers, maintainers, and tech experts to make the best technical decision.

To get started, see other users picks or start a new pick

In the core of Pick are the labels.
Label can be a library, programming language, technology or something completely different.
Pick is a decision between labels in a specific context and domain. A pick is usually subjective by its definition. It tires to help a user decide what is the best selection for her use case.
